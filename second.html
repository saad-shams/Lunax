Chapter One

THE SIGNAL

A story from the edge of known space.

01 // The Void

Silence

Deep in sector 7G, the silence isn't empty. It's heavy. It presses against the hull of your station like a physical weight. You've been monitoring the frequencies for three cycles.

02 // The Anomaly

Detection

A spike on the spectral analyzer. It's not background radiation. It's structured. Pulsing. A rhythm that feels too deliberate to be natural. It’s coming from the coordinates of the vanished colony.

03 // The Choice

Decryption

The AI warns of a viral payload embedded in the transmission. Opening it could compromise the entire station. But the signal contains images. Faces. The missing colonists are calling out. Do you risk the isolation protocol?

04 // Horizon

Connection

You bypass the firewall. The data floods your neural link. You see them, not in the past, but now. They aren't lost. They've evolved. They are the signal. And they've been waiting for you to listen.

End of Transmission
